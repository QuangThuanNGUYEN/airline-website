Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the baggage allowance?

Our international flights allow up to 30kg checked baggage and 7kg cabin baggage.

2. Can I change my flight date?

Yes, you can change your flight up to 24 hours before departure. Fees may apply.

3. How early should I arrive at the airport?

Please arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights.

4. Do you provide in-flight meals?

Yes, complimentary meals and drinks are provided on all international flights.

5. How can I contact customer service?

You can reach us via this page or call our 24/7 helpline at 1800-123-456.
